Burger

Home

Personal favorite, perfect for a weekend-food

Ingredients

  • Burger meat
  • Burger bread
  • Dressings
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove and fry the burger meet
  2. Heat the burger bread a little and add dressings (Mayoneese and Katchup)
  3. Slice the tomatoes and chop the lettuce, add them to the bread
  4. Place the burger meat inside the bread
  5. Enjoy!
